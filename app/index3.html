Pebblesss

A Pebble watch multiplayer interactive web game. Made in cloudpebble.net with Node.JS, using Websockets and HTML5 Canvas. A Java Script code hack. By the Students of FSU, UNC CH and NC A&T.


A snake game port from Higher Abstraction Level , redesigned to take in the functionality of pebbles.


How to play.
Use the buttons on your pebbles to control the direction of your colorful snake. Gobble up the blocks for food and grow your snake to anaconda proportions. Master your opponents and be the last snake standing.
